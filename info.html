Craig Stam(atelaky) (b. 1996) is a media artist from New York City. His recent work deals with the aestheticization of futures and nostalgic pasts through the performative aspects of games. He is currently trying discover a means to gain EXP and Level Up IRL.

He is also a part of the filipinx arts and design collective Export Quality.
